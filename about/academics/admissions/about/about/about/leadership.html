Leadership & Governance

Guiding Cornerstones Vision Academy with integrity, vision, and purpose.

Our Leadership Philosophy

At Cornerstones Vision Academy, leadership is rooted in service, accountability, and a deep commitment to student success. Our leadership team brings together experienced educators, administrators, and advisors who share a common vision: to create a safe, disciplined, and inspiring learning environment.

Decisions at CVA are guided by values, informed by educational best practices, and focused on long-term impact rather than short-term gain.

Leadership with Purpose

Every policy, program, and partnership is designed to support learning, character development, and community trust.

Governance & Administration

The governance structure of Cornerstones Vision Academy ensures clarity of roles, transparency in decision-making, and accountability at all levels. School leadership works closely with teachers, parents, and community stakeholders to maintain high standards and continuous improvement.

Through structured planning, regular evaluation, and open communication, the administration ensures that the academy remains aligned with its mission and responsive to the needs of its learners.

What Guides Our Leaders

  • Integrity – Ethical leadership and transparent decision-making.
  • Professional Excellence – Commitment to high standards in teaching and administration.
  • Collaboration – Working in partnership with families and the wider community.
  • Student-Centered Focus – Every decision prioritizes learner well-being and growth.

Partner With Our Leadership

We believe strong schools are built through trust, communication, and shared responsibility.
