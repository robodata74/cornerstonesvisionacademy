About Cornerstones Vision Academy

Nurturing academic excellence, character, creativity, and leadership for a brighter future.

Our Story

Cornerstones Vision Academy (CVA) was founded with a clear purpose: to provide quality education that goes beyond textbooks. From its early beginnings, the academy has grown into a learning community where academic excellence is balanced with moral values, cultural identity, and creative expression.

We believe that education should shape confident thinkers, responsible citizens, and compassionate leaders. Every program, activity, and classroom experience at CVA is designed to prepare learners not only for examinations, but for life.

Education Rooted in Purpose

At Cornerstones Vision Academy, learning is intentional, values-driven, and aligned with the future our students will lead.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that develops academically competent, morally upright, and socially responsible individuals capable of contributing meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that integrates academic rigor, creativity, cultural awareness, and character development in a supportive and inspiring learning environment.

Shaping Minds. Building Character.

Our mission is lived daily through teaching, mentorship, and community engagement.

Leadership & Governance

Cornerstones Vision Academy is guided by experienced educators and administrators who are committed to excellence, accountability, and student-centered learning.

Our leadership team works closely with teachers, parents, and the wider community to ensure that the academy remains a safe, disciplined, and progressive learning environment.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity – We uphold honesty, responsibility, and ethical behavior in all we do.
  • Excellence – We pursue high standards in academics, conduct, and leadership.
  • Creativity – We encourage innovation, self-expression, and critical thinking.
  • Community – We foster respect, collaboration, and unity among learners, staff, and families.
