About Cornerstones Vision Academy

A learning community dedicated to academic excellence, strong values, and leadership for life.

Our Story

Cornerstones Vision Academy (CVA) was established with a clear conviction: that education must shape both the mind and the character. From its founding, CVA has focused on delivering quality education grounded in discipline, creativity, and purpose.

What began as a vision has grown into a vibrant academic community where learners are encouraged to think independently, express themselves confidently, and engage responsibly with the world around them.

Education with Meaning and Direction

At CVA, learning is intentional — designed to prepare students not only for examinations, but for life.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that nurtures academically competent, morally upright, and socially responsible individuals capable of contributing positively to society.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that integrates academic excellence, character development, creativity, and cultural awareness within a safe, supportive, and inspiring learning environment.

Shaping Minds. Building Character.

Our philosophy is reflected in every classroom, activity, and interaction across the school community.

Leadership & Governance

Cornerstones Vision Academy is guided by a dedicated leadership team with extensive experience in education, administration, and student development.

Our leaders work collaboratively with teachers, parents, and stakeholders to ensure high standards, accountability, and a nurturing environment where learners can thrive.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We promote honesty, responsibility, and ethical conduct in all aspects of school life.

Excellence

We strive for high standards in academics, leadership, and personal growth.

Creativity

We encourage innovation, critical thinking, and self-expression through learning and the arts.

Community

We value respect, collaboration, and partnership among students, staff, parents, and the wider community.
