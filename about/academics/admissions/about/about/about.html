About Cornerstones Vision Academy

Building academic excellence, creativity, and leadership in South Sudan

Our Vision

Cornerstones Vision Academy seeks to be a leading educational institution in South Sudan, nurturing students who excel academically, embrace creativity, and demonstrate strong moral character.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide holistic education that balances academic rigor with cultural, artistic, and personal development, preparing students for future leadership and service.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethical behavior
  • Excellence: Striving for high standards in learning and conduct
  • Creativity: Encouraging innovation and artistic expression
  • Community: Fostering respect, unity, and teamwork
