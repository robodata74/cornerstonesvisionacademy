Primary School

Building strong academic and character foundations for lifelong learning.

A Strong Start to Learning

The Primary School at Cornerstones Vision Academy provides a safe, supportive, and engaging environment where young learners develop essential academic skills alongside confidence, curiosity, and good character.

Our approach emphasizes literacy, numeracy, inquiry-based learning, and positive values, ensuring that students are well prepared for the next stages of their education.

Learning Through Care and Structure

We believe young learners thrive best when they feel supported, encouraged, and challenged at the right pace.

Our Curriculum

The primary curriculum at CVA is designed to develop well-rounded learners through a balanced focus on core subjects and enrichment activities.

  • Language & Literacy – Reading, writing, and effective communication.
  • Mathematics – Numeracy, problem-solving, and logical thinking.
  • Science – Curiosity, exploration, and understanding the world.
  • Creative Arts – Art, music, drama, and self-expression.
  • Physical Education – Health, coordination, and teamwork.

Supporting Every Child

Teachers work closely with learners and parents to monitor progress, provide guidance, and celebrate achievements at every stage.
