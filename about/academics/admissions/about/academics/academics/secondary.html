Secondary School

Academic excellence, leadership development, and preparation for the future.

Preparing Students for the Next Stage

The Secondary School at Cornerstones Vision Academy is designed to challenge students intellectually while nurturing responsibility, independence, and leadership.

Through structured learning, mentorship, and high expectations, students are prepared for national examinations, further education, and meaningful roles in society.

Academic Rigor with Purpose

Learning at CVA encourages critical thinking, discipline, and confidence, preparing students to succeed in school, community, and beyond.

Academic Program

The secondary curriculum emphasizes depth of understanding, analytical skills, and application of knowledge across subjects, blending theory with real-world relevance.

  • Languages – Advanced literacy and communication skills for academic and social contexts.
  • Mathematics – Problem-solving, logical reasoning, and applied mathematics.
  • Sciences – Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and inquiry-based scientific exploration.
  • Humanities – History, geography, and civic responsibility to build global awareness.
  • Creative & Practical Subjects – Arts, technology, and innovation to foster creativity and applied skills.

Leadership & Personal Growth

Students are encouraged to take responsibility through leadership roles, clubs, community service, and extracurricular engagement. Our programs help develop self-confidence, teamwork, and ethical decision-making.
