Why Cornerstones Vision Academy?

A progressive, multicultural, value-based school shaping future leaders

By Valentino Achak Deng • January 2026

Cornerstones Vision Academy is a coeducational, progressive, multicultural, value-based day school located in Aweil, South Sudan. The academy offers high-quality General Education and Instruction Curriculum from early childhood through secondary school, positioning itself among the country’s top-tier learning institutions.

A defining strength of Cornerstones Vision Academy is its professional teaching team. Educators are highly qualified, passionate, and dedicated to creating a nurturing and intellectually stimulating learning environment. They employ innovative teaching strategies that accommodate diverse learning styles while remaining committed to continuous professional development.

The academy’s curriculum is carefully designed to promote critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving. It aligns with international academic standards while integrating social-emotional learning and interdisciplinary project-based approaches that prepare students for real-world challenges.

Modern learning facilities further enhance the educational experience. Spacious classrooms, science and computer laboratories, and technology resources foster hands-on exploration and discovery. These facilities empower students with the tools they need to excel academically and personally.

Leadership development is central to the academy’s philosophy. Cornerstones Vision Academy emphasizes ethical leadership through character education and community engagement. Parents can be confident that learners are being shaped into responsible, principled, and capable future leaders.
