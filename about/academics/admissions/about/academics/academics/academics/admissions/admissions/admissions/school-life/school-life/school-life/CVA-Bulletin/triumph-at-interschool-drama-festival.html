The Triumph of Cornerstones Vision Academy at the Interschool Drama Festival

Celebrating excellence in drama, folk dance, music, and poetry

By Valentino Achak Deng • January 2026

Cornerstones Vision Academy (CVA) has emerged as a champion of artistic excellence in the heart of Northern Bahr el Ghazal State, particularly evident in its remarkable achievements at the Interschool Drama Festival, which featured drama, folk dance, music, and poetry. This success can be attributed to the institution's dedication to nurturing students' creativity and talent. The academy's holistic approach to education, which emphasizes not just academic prowess but also the importance of the arts, has played a crucial role in shaping talented individuals who can express themselves and their culture through diverse artistic forms.

From the onset, Cornerstones Vision Academy has cultivated an environment where creativity flourishes. The administration and faculty have consistently supported initiatives to enhance the performing arts. This foundational support is instrumental in establishing a culture that values artistic engagement and encourages students to pursue their passions. In rehearsals and workshops leading up to the festival, educators provided guidance and mentorship, enabling students to explore their artistic identities. Such an environment not only sharpens students' skills but also instills confidence, a critical ingredient for successful performance.

The dramatic performances by students of Cornerstones Vision Academy are a testament to their dedication and talent. The students brought to life narratives that resonated with their audience, capturing the essence of their community and heritage. A careful selection of themes that reflected social issues pertinent to people of South Sudan allowed the participants to engage deeply with the content, resulting in performances that were both thought-provoking and entertaining. The commitment to authenticity in their artistic expressions demonstrated an appreciation for their cultural roots, fostering a sense of pride among performers and the audience alike.

In the realm of folk dance, Cornerstones Vision Academy’s students showcased a meticulous understanding of rhythm and tradition. By mastering dances that encapsulate the rich cultural fabric of their region, the academy's performers illustrated not only their technical ability but also deep respect for cultural meaning. The rehearsal process fostered teamwork, discipline, and mutual encouragement—values that extend well beyond the stage.

Musical presentations further underscored the academy's commitment to artistic excellence. Students delivered vocal and instrumental performances that blended traditional melodies with contemporary elements. These polished performances resonated deeply with audiences, demonstrating both technical skill and emotional expression. Poetry also thrived, as students articulated personal experiences, cultural narratives, and social justice themes with confidence and clarity.

In conclusion, Cornerstones Vision Academy's triumph at the Interschool Drama Festival reflects its unwavering commitment to arts education, cultural relevance, and holistic development. Through mentorship, collaboration, and excellence, CVA students showcased not only artistic talent but also leadership, confidence, and pride in identity. Their success stands as a model for institutions seeking to cultivate creativity alongside academic achievement.
