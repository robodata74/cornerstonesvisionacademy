School Fees

Transparent, structured, and student-focused.

Financial Transparency & Trust

At Cornerstones Vision Academy, we believe that clarity in financial obligations is key to building trust with our families. Our fees are structured to reflect quality education, holistic development, and sustainable support for all learners.

Primary School Fees

Fees are designed to cover academic programs, extracurricular activities, and basic student support.

Fee Type Amount (USD) Notes
Tuition $1,200 / year Inclusive of core curriculum classes
Books & Supplies $150 / year Textbooks, notebooks, art supplies
Uniform $100 / year Official school uniform set
Extracurricular Activities $200 / year Clubs, sports, arts, and cultural programs
Admission Fee $50 (one-time) Non-refundable registration fee

Investing in a Brighter Future

Every dollar paid supports quality education, enriched learning experiences, and the holistic development of our students.

Secondary School Fees

Fees cover advanced curriculum, leadership programs, and specialized support.

Fee Type Amount (USD) Notes
Tuition $1,500 / year Includes core academic programs and electives
Books & Supplies $200 / year Textbooks, lab supplies, and stationery
Uniform $120 / year Official school uniform set
Extracurricular Activities $250 / year Clubs, sports, arts, and cultural programs
Admission Fee $50 (one-time) Non-refundable registration fee
