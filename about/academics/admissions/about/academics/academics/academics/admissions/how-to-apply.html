Admissions Process

Simple. Transparent. Student-focused.

Welcome to Admissions

At Cornerstones Vision Academy, we welcome families who share our commitment to academic excellence, strong values, and holistic development.

Our admissions process is designed to be clear and supportive, ensuring that every student is placed in an environment where they can thrive.

How to Apply

  1. Step 1: Inquiry
    Contact the school or visit our campus to learn more about our programs and expectations.
  2. Step 2: Application
    Complete the official application form and submit all required documents.
  3. Step 3: Assessment
    Students may be invited for an academic assessment or interview, depending on grade level.
  4. Step 4: Admission Decision
    Families are notified of the admission outcome once the review process is complete.
  5. Step 5: Enrollment
    Upon acceptance, complete fee payment and registration to secure placement.
