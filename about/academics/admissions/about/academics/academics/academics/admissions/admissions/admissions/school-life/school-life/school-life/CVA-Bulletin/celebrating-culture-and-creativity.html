Celebrating Culture and Creativity

Triumph of Cornerstones Vision Academy during the Interschool Drama Festival in NBGS

By Valentino Achak Deng January 10, 2026

Cornerstones Vision Academy (CVA) has emerged as a champion of artistic excellence in the heart of Northern Bahr el Gazal State, particularly evident in its remarkable achievements at the Interschool Drama Festival, which featured drama, folk dance, music, and poetry. This success can be attributed to the institution's dedication to nurturing students' creativity and talent. The academy's holistic approach to education, which emphasizes not just academic prowess but also the importance of the arts, has played a crucial role in shaping talented individuals who can express themselves and their culture through diverse artistic forms.

From the onset, Cornerstones Vision Academy has cultivated an environment where creativity flourishes. The administration and faculty have consistently supported initiatives to enhance the performing arts. This foundational support is instrumental in establishing a culture that values artistic engagement and encourages students to pursue their passions. In rehearsals and workshops leading up to the festival, educators provided guidance and mentorship, enabling students to explore their artistic identities. Such an environment not only sharpens students' skills but also instills confidence, a critical ingredient for successful performance.

The dramatic performances by students of Cornerstones Vision Academy are a testament to their dedication and talent. The students brought to life narratives that resonated with their audience, capturing the essence of their community and heritage. A careful selection of themes that reflected social issues pertinent to people of South Sudan allowed the participants to engage deeply with the content, resulting in performances that were both thought-provoking and entertaining. The commitment to authenticity in their artistic expressions demonstrated an appreciation for their cultural roots, fostering a sense of pride among performers and the audience alike. This focus on culturally relevant storytelling is one of the academy's strengths, fostering connections that surpass the stage.

In the realm of folk dance, Cornerstones Vision Academy’s students showcased a meticulous understanding of rhythm and tradition. By meticulously learning dances that encapsulate the rich cultural textile of their region, the academy's performers illustrated not only their technical ability but also a deep respect for the cultural significance behind each movement. The rehearsal process was characterized by camaraderie and teamwork, as students worked collaboratively to perfect their routines. This collective effort not only enhanced their performance quality but also reinforced the values of cooperation and mutual support, essential traits that extend beyond the stage. The vibrancy of their performances captivated attendees, illustrating how dedication and practice can bring cultural heritage to life in a contemporary context.

Musical presentations at the interschool Drama Festival further underscored the academy's commitment to artistic excellence. Students engaged in vocal and instrumental performances that highlighted their diverse talents. The music selections blended traditional melodies and modern compositions, illustrating the students' versatility and innovation. Diligent practice sessions culminated in polished performances that resonated with the audience, engaging them in an auditory experience brimming with emotion and creativity. The success in music not only reflected the students' high level of talent but also showcased the academy's ability to effectively harness and direct that talent into memorable performances.

Poetry, another integral facet of the school's arts program, also thrived at the festival. Students crafted and delivered poems that explored personal experiences, cultural narratives, and social justice themes. The act of weaving words into evocative expressions facilitated self-exploration and showcased their literary talents. This endeavor fostered a strong sense of community as students encouraged one another, shared constructive feedback, and celebrated each other's successes. The powerful delivery of their poetry during the festival demonstrated the intersection of emotional intelligence and artistic expression, again reinforcing the idea that the school is committed to developing well-rounded individuals capable of articulating their thoughts and feelings effectively.

In conclusion, Cornerstones Vision Academy's triumph at the state’s Interschool Drama Festival can be attributed to its dedicated approach to arts education, which nurtures talent across drama, folk dance, music, and poetry. Through supportive mentorship, cultural relevance, cooperative learning, and a commitment to excellence, the students of Cornerstones Vision Academy not only showcased their artistic abilities but also forged a path of cultural pride and community engagement. Their achievements signify not just individual successes but also a collective affirmation of the importance of the arts in education, demonstrating how dedication and talent can indeed lead to triumph.
