Celebrating Culture and Creativity

Triumph of Cornerstones Vision Academy during the Interschool Drama Festival in NBGS

Valentino Achak Deng January 10, 2026

Cornerstones Vision Academy (CVA) has emerged as a champion of artistic excellence in the heart of Northern Bahr el Gazal State, particularly evident in its remarkable achievements at the Interschool Drama Festival, which featured drama, folk dance, music, and poetry. This success can be attributed to the institution's dedication to nurturing students' creativity and talent.

From the onset, Cornerstones Vision Academy has cultivated an environment where creativity flourishes. The administration and faculty have consistently supported initiatives to enhance the performing arts, enabling students to explore their artistic identities and build confidence.

The dramatic performances by students reflected authentic cultural narratives and social themes relevant to South Sudan. This commitment to storytelling fostered pride and deep audience connection.

Folk dance presentations demonstrated rhythm, discipline, and teamwork, bringing cultural heritage to life through vibrant movement and collaboration.

Musical and poetry performances further showcased students’ versatility, emotional intelligence, and creative expression, reinforcing CVA’s commitment to holistic education.

In conclusion, Cornerstones Vision Academy’s triumph at the Inter-school Drama Festival affirms the power of arts education in nurturing talent, cultural pride, and community engagement.
