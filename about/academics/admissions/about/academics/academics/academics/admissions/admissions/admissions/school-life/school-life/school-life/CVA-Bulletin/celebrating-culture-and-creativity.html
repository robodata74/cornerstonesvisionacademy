Celebrating Culture and Creativity: The Triumph of Cornerstones Vision Academy during the Interschool Drama Festival in NBGS

Cornerstones Vision Academy (CVA) has emerged as a champion of artistic excellence in the heart of Northern Bahr el Gazal State, particularly evident in its remarkable achievements at the Interschool Drama Festival, which featured drama, folk dance, music, and poetry. The academy’s holistic approach to education nurtures talent and creativity.

From the onset, CVA has cultivated an environment where creativity flourishes. The administration and faculty have consistently supported initiatives to enhance the performing arts, providing guidance and mentorship in rehearsals and workshops leading up to the festival.

The dramatic performances by CVA students captured narratives resonating with their audience, reflecting social issues pertinent to South Sudan, and fostering cultural pride.

Folk dance performances illustrated a deep respect for cultural traditions. Students worked collaboratively to perfect routines, demonstrating teamwork and dedication.

Musical presentations highlighted vocal and instrumental talents, blending traditional and modern compositions. Poetry allowed students to explore personal experiences and cultural narratives, showcasing emotional intelligence and creativity.

In conclusion, CVA’s success at the interschool Drama Festival demonstrates the value of arts education, mentorship, and cultural relevance. The achievements are a collective affirmation of the importance of the arts in education.

By Valentino Achak Deng
