School Events & Culture

Our Events

Cornerstones Vision Academy (CVA) hosts a variety of events throughout the academic year to celebrate student creativity, cultural heritage, and academic achievements. These include drama festivals, musical performances, folk dance exhibitions, and more.

Event & Culture Video

Through these events, CVA encourages students to explore their talents, work collaboratively, and develop confidence. Participation in interschool competitions and cultural activities reinforces the importance of teamwork, creativity, and community engagement.

We regularly update our events and cultural highlights in our CVA Bulletin for parents, students, and staff.
