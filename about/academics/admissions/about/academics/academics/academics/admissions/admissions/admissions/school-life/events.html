School Life at CVA

Celebrating culture, creativity, and community through vibrant student activities.

Culture & Events

Cornerstones Vision Academy is not just about academics; it’s a community where students express themselves through arts, music, drama, and cultural events. We host annual school festivals, cultural celebrations, and exhibitions that allow students to showcase their talents.

Clubs and Extracurricular Activities

We offer a wide range of clubs and activities that encourage leadership, teamwork, and personal growth. Students can join debate clubs, drama, art, music, sports teams, and STEM-related groups.

  • Debate Club – Develop critical thinking and communication skills.
  • Drama & Music – Performances, choir, and musical expression.
  • Sports Teams – Football, athletics, and inter-school competitions.
  • STEM Clubs – Coding, robotics, and science exploration.
  • Community Service – Leadership and social responsibility projects.

Learning Beyond the Classroom

At CVA, student life is designed to complement academics, fostering creativity, confidence, and collaboration.
